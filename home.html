About Us


Brood Africa allows anyone to identify and upload images, videos and contact details of any child suffering one form of abuse or the other between the ages of 6 and 17 anywhere in Africa. Brood Africa afterwards, reach out to the child, confirm if the child is genuinely in need, onboard the child and provide support in form of basic needs the child lacks till they can stand on their own.
