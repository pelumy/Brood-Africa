One in every 4 children is abused yearly in Africa and this abused is in a form of sexual, verbal, trafficking, hunger or labor. This leaves at least one form of negative impact or the other on the child till adulthood; and sometimes throughout their lifetime. Brood Africa allows anyone to identify and upload images, videos and contact details of any child suffering one form of abuse or the other between the ages of 6 and 17 anywhere in Africa. Brood Africa afterwards, reach out to the child, confirm if the child is genuinely in need, onboard the child and provide support in form of basic needs the child lacks till they can stand on their own. Brood Africa identifies the less priviledged children between the ages of 6 and 18 that are prone to one form of abuse or the other, then provides for and broods them with love and required resources till they are old enough to navigate life on their own. How It Works

Stage 1 - Identification

This the stage for identifying the target children that are more prone to one form of abuse or the other based on our assessment of their condition.

Stage 2 - Onboarding

This is the stage we register them, give them positive orientation and bring them under the umbrella of our solution.

Stage 3 - Brooding

Vision

Our vision is to protect millions of under-privileged African children between the ages of 6 and 18 from being affected by any form of abuse and brooding them with love.
