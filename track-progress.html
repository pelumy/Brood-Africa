Name name is a young boy who lives with his poor widowed mother in a remote village in Nigeria. The inability of his mother to feed them both has made him hawk roasted groundnuts all day long, which still doesn't take care of their needs. He rins after buses travelling through his state trying all possible means to sell his wares. Due to the rigorous labour he goes through daily without eating, he suffers from malnutrition. He has also been exposed to sexual abuse and lacks any form of education. He is a promising child with a bright future ahead, and that future cannot be bright without your help.

Your little contribution can go a long way to add meaning to little Name's life. Extend love to him and put a smile on his face today.
